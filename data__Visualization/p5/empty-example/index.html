Tone Of Language Amidst A Pandemic

Leaders of nations have addressed their citizens in response to the covid-19 pandemic. Despite the magnitude of the outbreak, political agenda remains prevalent in the actions and words of some leading figures.

The data visualization extracts repeated terminology from these speeches, starting with the most repeated word (Coronavirus) to the least (Pandemic). The amount of times a word is repeated is represented by the size of the colour coordinated arcs, each of which is assigned to a country.
